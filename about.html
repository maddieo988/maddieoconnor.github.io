Hello! My name is Maddie Oconnor! Im an 21 year old Animator and Artist currently enrolled at Marist University. I was born and raised in Anchorage, Alaska and I enjoy listening to all types of music, hanging with friends, taking care of plants, being outside, traveling, and creating art. I have also always loved movies, especially animated films, videos, shows etc and they have always inspired me which each ones unique qualities and characterisitcs. I hope to expand my artistic abilities and create and better my content as I progress and learn more. I specialize in a variety of other art as well which encourages me to be experimental in most artistic areas of my life!

I love trying new things with color, texture, and form, and my portfolio includes sculpture, painting, digital art, and more. My creative pieces are inspired by the world and people around me and a desire to invoke emotions and memories through my work for the viewers. I try to highlight the importance of seeing the beauty and good around you. It's important to remind oneself that there can be good, whether in the people you know, your environment, your experiences, your art etc.
